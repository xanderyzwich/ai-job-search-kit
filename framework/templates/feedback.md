## Feedback

Capture the feedback as it was given, in enough detail to make sense later
without the original conversation. Quote or paraphrase freely; this block is
the raw record, not yet filtered into decisions.

## Takeaways

Actionable items derived from the feedback — the "so what." Each should be a
concrete change or decision, not a restatement of the comment.
